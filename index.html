Privacy Policy for the Kind App

Effective Date: October 5, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Kind App ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our mobile application, Kind App (the "Service"). Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this policy outlines our commitment to protecting your personal data. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account Information: Email, password, display name.
  • Profile and Registration Data: Name, city, country, age, profession, motivation for kindness.
  • User-Generated Content: Journals, stamps, digital signature, friends info, shared content.

B. Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Data: IP address, device type, OS version, timestamps.
  • Device Information: Hardware model, unique identifiers.
  • Location Data: City, country when creating a "Moment".

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to provide, maintain, and improve our Service, manage accounts, enable core functionalities, facilitate sharing, send notifications, monitor usage, and enforce our policies.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your data. Sharing occurs only with:

  • Other users (when you share content explicitly)
  • Service providers (e.g., Firebase)
  • Legal requirements (court orders, government requests)

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally and on secure cloud servers (Firebase). We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no storage or transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Data Rights and Choices

  • Access and update profile info in the app.
  • Delete local content. Shared content may remain with recipients.
  • Request account deletion via support@kind.com.
  • Disable push notifications via device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on their practices.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here. Review periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at support@kind.com.
